Kinetic Typography

quick blurb here.

summary

Individual two-week project where students had to design posters for SIAT advisors to use when welcoming SIAT freshmen.

programs used

Adobe Illustrator

storyboards

My process began with making an initial storyboard of my initial ideas for feedback. After being told that I was heading in the right direction, I decided to take my ideas a step further by incorporating even more graphics to replace some of the text. I knew at the time that it was an ambitious decision since implementing so many graphics would add much more work to an already time-consuming project. However, it was a challenge I was willing to face so that I could do the iconic speech justice.

graphics & animating

With this storyboard as reference, I began creating the graphics in Adobe Illustrator and splitting the layers in Photoshop. This way they could be directly imported into After Effects where I would animate them alongside the text.

reflection

I initially took this class as an elective, so it was my first design course and the first time I had used Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects. This made working on this project quite a challenge. However, the more I put time into it, the more I realized that I was actually enjoying the process. This was one of the reasons why I ended up pursuing an IAT major.
