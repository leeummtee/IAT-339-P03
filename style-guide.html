Project Analyses

Project 1: Poster

For 103W’s final project we were required to create a poster for the SIAT advisors to use during welcome day for incoming students. These posters were to give these new SIAT students a piece of advice based on experience and insider information. For my poster I wanted to introduce Discord as a tool for making communication for group projects much easier.

In terms of the design process, I started out with multiple ideation sketches for a large banner-style poster with tips on getting started with Discord (Figure 1, 2, 3). We were required by the IAT 103W teaching team to incorporate signs and symbols, so I decided to make a design with the Discord logo wearing a cape to symbolize it as a powerful tool that can save the day. I also placed the program’s name on a shooting star to symbolize how Discord can be the answer to students’ wishes for an easier way to communicate on projects. I then made a higher fidelity mock-up in Adobe Illustrator (Figure 4).

My initial feedback from my peers was that a banner-style poster with all that information would be overwhelming for students to look at. Based on their critiques, I decided to shrink it down to an A0 size (841mm x 1189mm) by cutting out the steps and replacing it with a summary of Discord’s features (Figure 5).
